## Full Description (4000 char max)

SPLIT is the circuit training timer that gets out of your way and lets you train.

No more stopping and starting a stopwatch between exercises. No more losing count of your rounds. Build your circuit once, hit start, and the app runs the entire workout for you - hands-free.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick a preset or build your own circuit
2. Set exercises, work times, rest times, and rounds
3. Hit start - get a 3-2-1-GO countdown
4. Train. Audio beeps and vibration tell you when to switch.

DURING YOUR WORKOUT
- Green means WORK. Orange means REST.
- Massive countdown timer readable from across the gym
- Circular progress ring drains as each interval counts down
- Audio beeps count down the last 3 seconds of every interval
- Vibration pulses on every transition
- Skip rest when you're feeling it
- Pause anytime, pick up where you left off

BUILT FOR THE GYM
- Screen stays on during workouts
- Works offline - no internet needed at the gym
- Big, bold display you can read from metres away
- Save unlimited custom circuits
- 4 built-in presets: Full Body Blast, Tabata, Upper Body Circuit, Core Crusher

YOUR WORKOUTS, YOUR WAY
- Name each exercise
- Set individual work and rest times per exercise
- Set rounds and rest between rounds
- Reorder exercises easily
- Edit and duplicate saved workouts

No account needed. No ads. No tracking. Just a timer that works.
